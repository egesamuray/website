Research

I develop fast probabilistic imaging and dynamical-system models for scientific data. My work spans score-based generative models for inverse problems, simulation-based inference, and theory of learning dynamics in recurrent networks.

  • Generative modeling (diffusion/score-based); wavelet & curvelet priors
  • Simulation-based inference; efficient/conditional SGMs
  • Inverse problems & seismic imaging
  • Dynamical systems in ML: abrupt learning, working memory, phase transitions in RNNs
  • Trustworthy evaluation for one-to-many problems (e.g., SR)
